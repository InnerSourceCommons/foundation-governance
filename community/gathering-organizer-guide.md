# InnerSource Gathering Info
 
InnerSource Gatherings are community-led initiatives organized locally that bring together practitioners, advocates, and leaders interested in InnerSource. These gatherings are a place to initiate conversations about various issues in your organization in the common language of InnerSource.
 
The InnerSource Commons Foundation supports these efforts by:
 
- Amplifying gatherings through our communication channels
- Connecting organizers with the broader global community
- Sharing best practices and lessons learned across regions

### Interested in Organizing a Gathering?
 
We welcome community members who would like to host an InnerSource Gathering in their region. To get started, please complete our GitHub issue template to let the Foundation know about your plans and to receive guidance:
 
Review our [Gathering Organizer Guide](assets/gathering-organizer-guide.pdf) and [Submit a Gathering Proposal on GitHub](https://github.com/InnerSourceCommons/InnerSourceMarketing/issues)
 
The team will follow up to support you and help share your Gathering with the broader InnerSource Commons network.
 
As we continue to grow, we are exploring additional ways to better support local organizers and strengthen the global network of Gatherings. Get in touch at [info@innersourcecommons.org](mailto:info@innersourcecommons.org).
